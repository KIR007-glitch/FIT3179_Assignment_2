F1 SEASON 2021

The Most Intense Season in Decades

The Story So Far

The 2021 FIA Formula One World Competition was a motor racing championship for Formula One cars that took place for the 72nd time.

Max Verstappen of Red Bull Racing-Honda won the Drivers' Championship for the first time in his career at the season's finish in Abu Dhabi. Verstappen became the first Dutch driver to win the World Championship, the first Honda-powered driver since Ayrton Senna in 1991, the first Red Bull driver since Sebastian Vettel in 2013 , and the first non-Mercedes driver in the turbo-hybrid era.

G.O.A.T v UNDERDOG

"It was the championship tussle the Formula 1 world had long been waiting for and it certainly did not dissapoint."

Due to the intense competition between Lewis Hamilton and Max Verstappen, the 2021 F1 season will go down in the history books as one of the most significant and nail biting seasons. These two drivers' point difference has maintained a relatively narrow separation from the start of the season, leaving no room for mistake. Tragically, despite several disputes that developed during the campaign, the F1 title was decided on the very last lap of the last circuit.

What happened in Abu Dhabi?

Verstappen and Hamilton were equal on points heading into the last race of the 2021 season, which was a winner-take-all affair. In the final circuits, Hamilton maintained his advantage, with Verstappen in second position.

Nicholas Latifi of Williams crashed at turn 14 to bring out the safety car. Verstappen reacted by pitting for new soft tyres, but Hamilton continued on his old hard tyres.

As the laps ticked down, the stewards originally planned to have no lapped cars pass the safety car, unlike the normal procedure. Red Bull team boss Christian Horner asked why lapped cars werenâ€™t allowed to pass.

On the penultimate lap, race director Michael Masi decided to allow lapped cars through, but even more controversially, only the five cars between Hamilton and Verstappen. Immediately after Sebastian Vettel passed the safety car, the stewards announced the safety car would come in to allow one final lap of racing. On the last lap of the race and the season,Verstappen used his new tyres to his advantage and passed Hamilton at turn 5.

2021 Season in Summary

Is there a relationship between the grid position, Final Position and the Fastest Lap ?


One of the most evident factors in the 2021 season was that the grid Position was highly correlated with the final Position and the fastest Lap, this meant that the drivers who managed to obtain a better starting position (Grid Position) were able to end up at a better Final Position and set the record for the fastest Lap

What decides a World Championship ?

"It is often known that to be better at something you need to keep doing it frequently and as often as you can."

This is true for the sport of Formula 1. By analyzing the data visualization below it can be seen that Mercedes and Redbull had the drivers with the most amount of combined experience among themselves thereby far outweighing other constructors in terms of experience.

Hence it is no wonder why these two constructors were the favorites for the world championship , going into the 2021 season. Hamilton and Bottas had a total combined experience of 466 Grand Prix Races among them where as Max Verstappen and Sergio Perez had a total combined experience of 355 Grand Prix races.
